Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup Minced Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5oz) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp parsley
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 0.5 tsp fennel seeds
  • 0.5 tsp black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 16 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 lb. Mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan
  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tbsp parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer while covered for 1.5 hours stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook Lasagna for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles and Rinse with cold water.
  4. In mixing bowl, combine ricotta, with egg, remaining parsley and 1/2 tsp salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Assemble lasagna. Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce on bottom of a 9x13. Arrange 6 noodles over. Then add half ricotta mixture. Top with 1/3 mozzarella slices. Spoon 1.5 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with .25 cup of parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers, top with remaining mozzarella and cover with foil. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  8. Rest for 15 minutes before serving.
